What is Paranormal Activity?

Paranormal activity refers to unexplained phenomena beyond scientific understanding. These include ghost sightings, poltergeist disturbances, shadow figures, and even strange energies sensed by people. Many cultures believe spirits of the dead roam the Earth, interacting with the living. Some claim these encounters are proof of an afterlife, while others argue they are psychological illusions. From haunted houses to chilling possessions, paranormal events have fascinated and terrified humanity for centuries.

Common Paranormal Activities:

  • Ghostly Apparitions: Seeing spirits of deceased individuals.
  • Poltergeist Disturbances: Objects moving, doors slamming on their own.
  • Electronic Voice Phenomena (EVP): Mysterious voices captured on recordings.
  • Shadow People: Dark figures lurking in the corners of vision.
  • Possessions: Alleged control of a person by a spirit or demon.
